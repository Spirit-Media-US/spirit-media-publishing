Are You a Kingdom Messenger?

You carry a message from God that has been refined through years of prayer, crushing, and surrender. You've seen enough fruit to know it has power to transform lives. But you don't just see a message — you see a movement.

The question is: how do you move from message to movement?

The Program

From Message to Movement

When Jesus said "Come, follow me," He wasn't inviting people to hear a talk — He was inviting them into a movement. The Kingdom Messenger Collective is our flagship partnership program, designed to walk with you through a 3+ year journey from restoration to proclamation, and from message to sustainable global movement.

This isn't coaching. It's a covenant relationship. You bring the message. Spirit Media provides the platform, the strategy, and the team.

Your Mentor

Kevin White

Kevin White is an international bestselling author of seven books in five years. His first title, Audacious Generosity, was a bestseller in India before reaching the USA. His second book has over 2 million downloads in Nepal.

Through weekly video podcasts over 7+ years, Kevin has reached audiences in 163 countries. As founder and CEO of Spirit Media, he has helped publish over 300 books for authors across the globe.

For over 40 years, God has used Kevin to raise up and commission Kingdom Messengers. Now, he invites you to walk the same path.

Soul Care

Messenger Soul Care

Weekly Private Calls

30-minute sessions with Kevin White. Father-focused development through prayer, discernment, and counsel for family, business, and ministry.

Monthly Mastermind

Iron sharpens iron. Gather with fellow Kingdom Messengers for encouragement, accountability, and shared wisdom.

Ongoing Access

Direct connection to Kevin for advice and encouragement between sessions.

Quarterly Reviews

Dedicated time to assess goals, celebrate progress, and chart next steps for your movement.

Pathway

The Formation Pathway

Phase 1 — Heart

Restoring the Father/Child Relationship

Intimacy with Father. Identity from Father. Inheritance through Father. We prioritize His work in you — becoming the healthiest adult version of yourself through inner healing, forgiveness, freedom, and transformational prayer ministry.

Phase 2 — Message

Entering the Kingdom

Discovering the Kingdom through Father's heart, through brokenness, and through community. Living God's Word with discipline, generosity, and celebration. Stepping into your role as king, priest, and minister.

Phase 3 — Movement

Doing the Stuff

Restoration. Healing. Deliverance. Transformation. Taking everything the Father has built in you and releasing it through you — to the nations.

Journey

Year 1

Year 2

Growth

Multiple books, expanding audience, new revenue streams, deepening influence.

Year 3

Movement

Train leaders, expand globally, develop legacy systems.

Beyond

Multiplication

Generational impact. Global Kingdom influence.

Infrastructure

Movement-Building Services
