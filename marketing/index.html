Services

Marketing Services

Book Marketing: Amazon Reviews

Six-month partnership focused on increasing Amazon reviews. Includes custom instruction sheet, systematic implementation, with a goal of 25 reviews per month.

Book Marketing: Advance Reader Copies

We send advance copies to your personal contacts and USA bookstores. Per-copy cost covers mailing list, up to $5 toward printing, fulfillment, and postage.

Book Marketing: Paid Digital Ads

Twelve-month partnership with setup and monthly optimization of paid ads on one platform: Amazon, Google, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok.

Websites with SEO

High-performance, responsive website built on the Astro + Tailwind + Claude stack — SSL, SEO, structured data, accessibility, lead gen forms, analytics, and AI-assisted updates in plain English. No technical skills needed.

Landing Pages

High-converting, responsive landing page built on the Astro + Tailwind + Claude stack — optimized headline, lead capture form, CTA, SSL, SEO, and AI-assisted updates in plain English. No technical skills needed.

CRM Platform

Setup and monthly optimization of GoHighLevel (Spirit Media ONE) — conversations, calendar scheduling, business phone, voicemail, SMS, invoicing, payment processing, sales pipeline, forms, funnels, workflows, and automations.

Community Platform

Setup and monthly optimization of a branded online community including structure, content strategy, and member development.

Video Production

Eight hours of professional filming — story development, scripting, and full post-production — delivered as short-form feature videos and up to 20 reels per month for 12 months.

Podcast Production

Setup of a podcast tour with pitch sheet and scheduling management — or launch your own show with full weekly production across all major platforms.

YouTube Channel Optimization

Twelve-month partnership in strategy, publishing, and channel optimization for one YouTube channel.

Social Media Management

Twelve-month partnership managing up to three platforms. Includes content creation, scheduling up to 20 posts per month, and weekly community engagement.

Email Marketing

Twelve-month partnership in email design and management — up to 4 emails per month to your contact list.

SMS Marketing

Twelve-month partnership in SMS marketing — up to 4 messages per month to your contact list.

Public Relations

Press releases, media kits, promotional videos, flyers, and pitch decks. North America distribution through PR Newswire.

Google Ads for Nonprofits

Analytics & Reporting

Monthly tracking of book sales, website traffic, YouTube, podcasts, and social media performance.
