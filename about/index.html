About Us

Why We
Exist

Spirit Media exists because the Good News was never meant to stop at borders. Too often, vision in publishing has been small, limited to markets instead of nations. But our Heavenly Father is worthy of His story bearing fruit in all 195 nations of the earth. We exist to remove the barriers — the complexity, the cost, the confusion — and to equip Kingdom Messengers with the publishing and marketing solutions they need to multiply Kingdom impact and generational inheritance.

What began as holy frustration became a global mandate. We are here to build sustainable Kingdom movements, amplify our Father's heart, and see His whisper reach every tribe, tongue, and nation.

Leadership

Kevin & Shelly White

Kevin White is an author, speaker, and marketplace pastor with decades of experience planting churches, leading nonprofits, starting companies, and developing leaders worldwide. Together with his wife, Shelly, he co-founded and leads Spirit Media, Father's Heart Bible™, and Work On Yourself.

Our Mission

Our Vision

Our Values

This is the culture that carries that mission.

Father's Heart

Everything flows from who God is as Father.

We seek to reveal the Father Heart of God in every relationship and decision. We refuse orphan thinking. We build from intimacy, identity, and inheritance, not insecurity. We lead with love, truth, clarity, and courage. Every author, every partner, every team member is seen, valued, and called higher. We build people — not just platforms.

Faith

We move where vision requires dependence on God.

We pursue bold vision, trusting our perfect and loving Heavenly Father for outcomes beyond our capacity. If it can be done without Him, it isn't big enough. We embrace bold goals, global reach, and supernatural outcomes. We expect fruit beyond strategy, impact beyond borders, and provision beyond logic. We build with faith — not fear.

Family

We operate from covenants, not contracts.

We build covenant relationships and treat every author, partner, and team member like family. Honor and trust matters here. We celebrate together. We carry one another. We speak truth in love and protect unity fiercely. Culture is not what we say — it's how we treat each other when it costs something.

Excellence

Our Father deserves our best as we seek His best.

We reject mediocrity. We refine, sharpen, improve, and elevate. From manuscript to marketplace, we steward every detail with intentionality. Excellence is worship in action.

Multiplication

We think nations, not numbers.

We are not building for one launch or one market. We empower heart → message → movement that multiply intimacy with the Father, beloved identity, and kingdom inheritance across generations. The mandate is 195 nations — and we build accordingly.

Stewardship

Nothing entrusted to us is small.

Messages carry eternity. Resources carry responsibility. Relationships carry legacy. We handle them with integrity, wisdom, and long-term Kingdom vision.
